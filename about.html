Athletics

With a strong foundation in both electrical engineering and high-performance athletics, I am well-accustomed to perseverance and thrive on tackling challenges head-on. Having spent 17 years honing my skills in swimming, including 10 years competing at the Canadian National Level, overcoming adversity has become second nature to me. During my five years as a swimmer at the University of Ottawa, I not only earned multiple conference medals but also played a pivotal role in helping the university secure its first-ever national banner. This experience has solidified my commitment to excellence, both in the pool and beyond. Excellence in motion—unwavering and relentless. Strive today, Succeed tommorrow!!
